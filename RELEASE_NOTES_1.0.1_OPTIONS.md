## Pro Tips for Future Updates

### Do's

- ✅ Lead with user-visible fixes
- ✅ Mention performance but keep it brief
- ✅ Thank users for feedback
- ✅ Use emoji sparingly (makes it scannable)
- ✅ Keep it under 500 characters for readability
- ✅ Be specific about what changed

### Don'ts

- ❌ Don't be too technical (most users don't care about implementation)
- ❌ Don't list every single change (group related fixes)
- ❌ Don't use marketing speak ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
- ❌ Don't exceed 1000 characters (users won't read it)
- ❌ Don't mention bugs that users haven't noticed
