# App Store Description - Molten

## Short Description (Subtitle)
Run LLMs Offline, Private

## Full Description

**Molten - Local AI. On Your Terms.**

Molten is a privacy-first app that runs local AI language models completely offline. Chat with LLMs like Llama, Qwen, and more—no internet required, no data collected.

### 🌟 Why Molten?

Unlike other AI apps that send your data to the cloud, Molten keeps everything on your device. Your conversations never leave your Mac, iPhone, or iPad.

**Perfect for:**
- Privacy-conscious users
- Developers testing local models
- Offline AI access
- Sensitive conversations
- Learning about local LLMs

### ✨ Key Features

**Multi-Provider Support**
Connect to Ollama, Swama, or Apple Foundation Models—all in one elegant interface. Switch between providers seamlessly.

**100% Offline**
All processing happens on your device. No internet connection needed after models are loaded. No data leaves your device. Ever.

**Native Apple Design**
Built with SwiftUI for macOS, iOS, and iPadOS. Feels at home on every Apple device with dark mode, keyboard shortcuts, and responsive design.

**Real-Time Streaming**
Watch responses stream in real-time with performance metrics showing tokens per second, prompt eval rate, and more.

**Conversation History**
Persistent conversations with SwiftData. Pick up where you left off anytime.

**Voice & Text-to-Speech**
Use voice input for prompts and have responses read aloud with system voices.

**Markdown Rendering**
Beautiful rendering of formatted text, code blocks with syntax highlighting, tables, and more.

### 🔒 Privacy Promise

- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No tracking
- ❌ No identifiers

- ❌ No third-party sharing

Your data stays on your device. Period.

### 📋 Requirements

**To use Molten, you need:**

1. **Apple Silicon Mac** (M1, M2, M3, or later) for Apple Foundation Models
2. **At least one model provider** (choose any):
   - **Ollama** (free) - Download at https://ollama.ai
   - **Swama** (free) - Optimized for Apple Silicon
   - **Apple Foundation Models** - Built-in on macOS 26.0+

**Note**: The app itself is free and collects no data. Model providers are separate, free, open-source projects.

### 🎹 Keyboard Shortcuts (macOS)

- ⌘↩ - Send message
- ⌘⌥K - Toggle panel mode
- ⌘, - Open Settings
- ⌘N - New conversation

### 💡 Getting Started

1. Install Ollama or Swama (free)
2. Download a model (e.g., `ollama pull llama2`)
3. Open Molten
4. Select your model and start chatting!

Detailed setup instructions in the app's Settings panel.
